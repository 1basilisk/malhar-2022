The Quartet

The task of rerouting the legacy in the first-ever virtual Malhar lies in the hands of the Big Four. Q’21 is a melange of passion, potential, pretence and tons of humour. They’re ones to kickstart fest preparations and are also the last ones to sign off. The ones functioning virtually; the quartet seeks to garnish this Malhar with loads of positivity and the same amount of hype and enthusiasm as it has ever been.
